What We Do

tips_and_updates Brand Identity Development

Our work with new brands encompasses everything from naming and developing the brand's perspective, to establishing its position and defining its visual identity - including elements such as the logo, colour scheme, typography, and how these aspects are applied across different contexts.

groups Social

When it comes to brands' social media mandates, our work includes social strategy, creator-led video first content, conceptualizing temporal campaigns and handling campaign roll-outs, perfomance management and influencer marketing.

movie_edit Content Production

We cater to the specific video content requirements for brands, which could include producing content for podcasts, in-house films, case studies, corporate videos, training videos, or any kind of audio production. We also have extensive tie-ups that enable us to work across 9 Indian languages and 40 Asian languages.

movie_filter Video Production

We offer diverse video production services, spanning across styles like 2D/3D animations, stop motion, post-production videos, motion graphics, and live-action filming.

view_in_ar Extended Reality

Our Extended Reality (XR) expertise span across Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and include 360° stereoscopic video production. In the AR sphere, our capabilities extend to Spark (Instagram), Lens Studio (Snapchat), and WebAR (Platform Agnostic). For VR, we develop applications on Unity for the Oculus and other devices.

psychology Strategy

Our brand strategy services can be employed at both the brand and campaign launch level, or to address specific marketing problems defined by the brand. Typically, our strategic services are complemented by execution mandates.

devices_other UI/ UX

We undertake research-driven design projects, commencing with user research, wireframing, rapid prototyping, high-fidelity designs on Figma, and user testing, to ultimately deliver user-friendly designs.

deployed_code Web Development

Our proficiency in full-stack web development covers responsive web design, e-commerce/ Shopify websites, on-page and off-page SEO, as well as integrating additional functionalities such as chatbots.

palette Design and Collateral

We collaborate with clients on standalone design projects, which could include creating large brochures, calendars, coffee table books, or predetermined collateral packages.
