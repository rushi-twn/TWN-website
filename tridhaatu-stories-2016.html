Bringing Lord Ganesha's stroies
alive in a true-to-life execution.

Background

The calendar brief has always been a favorite at Think WhyNot for the sheer chance to think differently with absolutely the same brief.

Challenge

The challenge as every year was to create yet another differentiated execution to depict the Lord.

Idea

We have all heard of different stories of Lord Ganesha. Yet, most of these stories have remained in our imagination. A few cinematic depictions have been done in movies and television serials. This year we decided to create a calendar to bring alive the stories and myths of Lord Ganesha in live action. The challenge was to imagine and then create different worlds and action sequences the way it happened in the Vedic times. More complicated was how we would create Lord Ganesha's face. Should we use prosthetics or 3D for the same?

We researched various sources including the Ganesh Puran and finally arrived at how we would visualize each story. The worlds were created using a combination of CG and matte paintings and it was decided to create the lord's face in 3D. The various characters were played by live models. Three models were needed for Lord Ganesha himself, since there were stories of Lord Ganesha of the various stages of his life.
